Our Contribute to Charities

Bool Street Noods has at heart helping others. Even though we are a small business we still hope to make a change in our little.

All profits as a matter of fact go to a different cause every week! We have cumulatively donated over 2000 dollars so far to Operation Hope, the Breast Cancer Research Foundation, and more!

Here you can find all the charities we are helping out if you are interested in contributing on your own to these causes.

List of Charities:

BCRF

The Breast Cancer Research Foundation is an independent, not-for-profit organization which has raised $569.4 million to support clinical and translational research on breast cancer at medical institutions in the United States and abroad. BCRF currently funds over 275 researchers in 15 countries.

Operation Hope

This charity, delivered by Financial Wellbeing Coaches, is here to EDUCATE you on what you need to know, COACH you through putting what you learn into practice and CONNECT you to the resources you need to keep doing it. Operation HOPE has a series of programs to support your path to successfully managing your money, owning a home, starting a business, or recovering from a disaster (hurricane, tornado, fire, pandemic, etc.). In addition to working with adults, it also offer a program to support youth.

Planned Parenthood

The Planned Parenthood Federation of America, Inc., or simply Planned Parenthood, is a nonprofit organization that provides reproductive health care in the United States and globally.

Center for Reproductive Rights

The Center for Reproductive Rights is the only global legal advocacy organization dedicated to advancing reproductive rights. Learn about our landmark court victories, legal policy and advocacy work, resources and more. Working with local partners, the Center strengthens reproductive health laws and policies in Africa, Asia, Europe, Latin America and the Caribbean, and the United States.
